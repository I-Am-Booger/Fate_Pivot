HOW TO PLAY
Your Mission
Don’t let the board explode, implode, overload, or generally ruin your day.

1. You Are the Diamond Block!
See that fancy block with the diamond? That’s you.
You can’t walk anywhere, but you can magically respawn like you’re late for work.

Press R1 before the timer yells at you.

2. The Selector
This is your little cursor buddy.

It only moves up, down, left, right.

No diagonals. It’s allergic.

Put it over a block to threaten it with destruction.

3. Destroying Blocks
Every block shows a specific face button.

Hit the right one → block goes poof.

Hit the wrong one → a bad block appears and the board speeds up like it had too much coffee.

4. Losing
You lose points if you forget to respawn.
You lose the whole game if:

You hit –50, or

The grid fills up and everything becomes sadness.

5. Combos
Delete three same‑color blocks in a row and boom — combo time.
Fancy effects, big energy, good vibes.

6. Purple Blocks
Purple blocks are chaos gremlins.
They might help you.
They might ruin your life.
Roll the dice.

Good luck! Try not to explode anything important.
